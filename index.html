Brazil

Brazil, the largest country in South America, is known for its diverse culture, vibrant festivals, and natural wonders. It is home to the Amazon Rainforest, one of the most biodiverse places on Earth, and famous landmarks like Christ the Redeemer in Rio de Janeiro. Brazil's population is a blend of Indigenous, European, African, and Asian influences, reflected in its rich traditions, music, and cuisine. Portuguese is the official language, and the country is renowned for its passion for football (soccer), having won the FIFA World Cup five times. With sprawling cities like São Paulo and Rio alongside breathtaking landscapes, Brazil offers a unique blend of urban and natural beauty.
